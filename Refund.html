Refund Policy

1. Overview

At ShieldZone, customer satisfaction is our priority. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on most of our products, ensuring a risk-free experience for our customers.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

You are eligible for a refund if:

• The request is made within 30 days of purchase
• The product has not been misused or violated licensing terms
• You are unable to activate or use the product due to a valid issue

3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds may not be provided in the following cases:

• Change of mind after successful activation
• Violation of product license terms
• Requests made after the 30-day refund period
• Issues caused by user device incompatibility not mentioned in product details

4. Refund Process

To request a refund, please contact our support team with your order details and reason for the request.

Once your request is reviewed and approved, the refund will be processed to your original payment method.

5. Processing Time

Approved refunds are typically processed within 5–10 business days, depending on your payment provider.

6. Technical Issues

If you experience any technical issues, we strongly recommend contacting our support team before requesting a refund. Our team is available 24/7 to help resolve any problems quickly.

7. Chargebacks

Initiating a chargeback without contacting us first may delay the resolution process. We encourage customers to reach out to our support team for faster assistance.

8. Changes to Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Refund Policy at any time without prior notice. Changes will be posted on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding refunds, please contact us through our website or support email.
